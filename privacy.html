Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Tobin Science is built by a practicing classroom teacher, and protecting student privacy comes first. Our tools are designed to collect as little data as possible. This policy explains, in plain language, what we do and don't collect.

Student privacy: we keep students anonymous

  • Students do not create accounts and do not enter personal information to use our products.
  • Teachers hand out reusable access codes, and students play using those codes. We do not collect student names, emails, or other personally identifiable information.
  • Where a game includes an optional leaderboard, students choose a display name or initials that are not tied to any real identity, used only to show game rankings.

Tools that stay on your device

Some tools, such as the Class Manager, store all information locally in the teacher's own web browser. That data stays on the device, is never transmitted to us, and is cleared when the teacher clears it.

Teacher accounts and purchases

When a teacher buys a product, we store only the teacher's email address so we can deliver access and provide support. We use trusted third-party services to run the site securely:

  • Stripe processes payments. We never see or store full payment card details.
  • Supabase securely stores account information, access codes, and game leaderboard entries.
  • YouTube hosts our embedded videos; watching them is subject to Google/YouTube's own privacy policy.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.
  • We do not use advertising networks, and we run no analytics or tracking scripts on our pages.
  • We do not share data with third parties for marketing.

Children's privacy (FERPA & COPPA)

Because students use our products anonymously and we do not collect student personal data, our tools are designed to help teachers and schools stay compliant with student data privacy laws such as FERPA and COPPA.

Questions

If you have any questions about this policy or your data, please reach out through the contact form at tobinscience.com.

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
